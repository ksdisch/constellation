- **Acceptance:** An in-game 🔊/🔇 toggle on **Hub and Planet** (pinned, ≥44px, hand cursor) flips a single master mute that **both** engines honor — SFX and music go silent — while the bridge keeps RECORDING cues/tracks (the "requested ≠ audible" split M5 already had). Default unmuted; the choice **persists across reload** via a versioned/guarded/never-throws `localStorage` twin of `save.ts` (own key `constellation:settings`), applied on boot before any scene plays audio. Freeze Stars + planet-1 unaffected.
- **Size:** S
- **Added:** 2026-06-14
- **Completed:** 2026-06-14
